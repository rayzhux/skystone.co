Who we are

Skystone Partners ("Skystone," "we," "us") operates the website skystone.co from Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

What data we collect

This website does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or third-party analytics scripts. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service that does not track individual visitors, does not use cookies, and does not collect personally identifiable information.

When you visit our site, Cloudflare may process limited technical data (such as page URL, referrer, browser type, and country) in aggregate form only. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Email correspondence

If you contact us via contact@skystone.co, we retain your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your information with third parties.

Third-party services

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Workers and uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect limited data (such as your IP address) when serving font files; see Google's privacy policy for details.

Your rights

Under applicable data protection law (including UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact us at contact@skystone.co.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
