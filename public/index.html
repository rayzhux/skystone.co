§ 01 · About

A bridge between the Gulf
and China's top builders.

Skystone exists to do one thing well: bring the capability of China's leading construction and development firms into real estate work across the Gulf. The work is retained, private, and selective.

I've built in Dubai for a decade — at TRADE X, on placement at the Prime Minister's Office of the UAE, and as a founding member of Dubai Future Foundation. I'm a native Mandarin speaker, with working relationships inside China State Construction, China Harbour, Vanke, and their peers that make cross-border execution actually land.

Skystone advises principals and family offices on acquisition, development, and asset strategy — and handles the work end-to-end so clients don't have to.

— Raymond Zhu, managing director

§ 02 · Services

Six disciplines, held to a single standard.

01

Investment Advisory

Acquisition, disposition, and repositioning strategy. Underwriting memos, hold-period analysis, and sourcing across Dubai and the wider GCC.

02

Asset & Portfolio Strategy

Annual portfolio reviews with hold, reposition, or divest recommendations — benchmarked against market and the principal's horizon.

03

Development Feasibility

Site assessment, programme testing, and pro forma underwriting before design begins. Chinese construction partners engaged early for cost and buildability input.

04

Owner’s Representation

Representation from first sketch through handover, aligned with the principal's interest — managing architects, contractors, and consultants so the project is delivered as specified.

05

Transaction Management

Diligence, negotiation, and closing on off-market and marketed deals. Discretion throughout; nothing public without client consent.

06

Strategic Insights

Periodic notes on Gulf real estate and China–GCC capital flows, written for clients.
